Mug Omelette

  • Home

A fast, single-serving omelette made in a mug. No stovetop needed.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 2 tablespoons shredded cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped ham or pre-cooked veggies
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a microwave-safe mug. Add the milk and beat with a fork.
  2. Stir in the cheese, ham, and a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute. Stir the mixture with a fork.
  4. Microwave for another 60-90 seconds, or until the eggs are fully set.
  5. Let it sit for 1 minute before tipping it onto a plate. Enjoy!
