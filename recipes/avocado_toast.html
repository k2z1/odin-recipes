Avocado Toast

  • Home

Easy, healthy avocado toast recipe. A quick no-cook breakfast or snack with mashed avocado on toasted bread, seasoned to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • red pepper flakes, everything bagel seasoning, or a squeeze of lemon juice

Steps

  1. Toast your slice of bread to your liking.
  2. While the bread toasts, slice the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork to your preferred consistency (chunky or smooth).
  4. Spread the mashed avocado evenly onto the warm toast.
  5. Season generously with salt, pepper, and any other toppings you like.
