Banana Pancake

  • Home

Simple banana and egg pancakes recipe. A quick, healthy breakfast idea with no flour, ready in minutes. Perfect for using ripe bananas.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 large eggs
  • pinch of cinnamon or a few chocolate chips

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mash the banana with a fork until smooth.
  2. Crack in the eggs and whisk everything together until well combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat with a little oil or butter.
  4. Pour small amounts of the batter to form pancakes. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the edges look set.
  5. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes, until golden brown. Serve immediately.
